Event Information :-

• In 2004 IEEE STB had the first inter-college Annual event INFINITY .

• 2006 onwards IEEE-GIT started its annual flagship event Paanchajanya(PCH).An event which attracted Delegates from Sangli, Kolhapur, Goa, Bengaluru, Hyderabad.

• PCH 2015 celebrated 30yrs of initiation of a Student chapter at GIT. (20yrs of being active since 1995-96) and 10yrs of PCHDr. D H Rao, Prof. Hemant Kamat, Dr. Raghavendra V Kulkarni, Dr. G R Udupi, Prof V P Gejji were felicitated.

• Student Leadership Program(SLP) :- It is a whole day event with the most interesting activities which improves communication skills,helps participants to become a social person. This event also helps in business as well as marketing skills through the activities like product designing and selling the self-made product.event helps in overall personality development of an individual.

• IEEE SB was a part of NK Hub-level event i-GREEN organized in 2014 -Theme global warming.

• The WIE Affinity group IEEE Student branch celebrates Women's Day since 2003initiallycalled as TRIGARTI now called as UDAAN!.
